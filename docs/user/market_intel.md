# Stock Rankings — User Guide

## What does it do?
Combines all the dashboard's signals into one ranked list of the most compelling bullish and bearish setups right now — so you don't have to check each feature individually. Think of it as the dashboard's "best ideas" list.

## Tabs

### Ranked Picks
Two columns: **Top Bullish** and **Top Bearish** stocks based on a composite score from:
- Options flow (P/C ratio, IV rank, squeeze signals)
- Reversal signal (technical momentum)
- Insider buying/selling activity

**Time horizons:**
- **1W** — Short-term signals (1-week outlook)
- **1M** — Medium-term (1-month) — the most reliable
- **3M** — Longer-term positioning

Each pick shows the score, which signals fired, current price, and 1-day change.

**Higher score = more signals aligned = more conviction.**

### Smart Money
Stocks where multiple "smart money" signals are stacked together:
- Institutions accumulating (13F flow)
- Insiders buying (Form 4)
- Congress members purchasing (STOCK Act)
- Unusual options activity (large premium bets)

A stock appearing in Smart Money has at least 2 of these simultaneously — institutional + insider confirmation, for example.

### Market Overview
The macro backdrop:
- S&P 500, Nasdaq, Russell 2000 — where are the major indices?
- VIX — market fear level
- Sector performance — which sectors are leading/lagging?
- Market breadth — is the rally broad or narrow?

Use this tab before making any trades to understand what kind of market you're in.

## How to use Ranked Picks
1. Check the **Market Overview** first — are we in a bull or bear trend?
2. Look at the **Ranked Picks** for the 1M horizon — these are the most balanced signals
3. Cross-reference top picks against the **Options Analysis** overview tab for confirmation
4. Check if the stock has upcoming **Earnings** in the next 30 days — binary events can override signals

## What the score means
Scores range from 0–10. Think of it as "how many strong signals are aligned":
- **7–10**: Multiple strong signals — high conviction
- **4–6**: Moderate — worth watching
- **0–3**: Weak signal — may be noise

The dashboard automatically updates these rankings every ~25 minutes during market hours.
